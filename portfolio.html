1. Aplicación Web para la Simplificación de las funciones relacionadas al Trabajo Terminal dentro de la ESCOM | ESCATT

    Project description:
  • Website to assist the student community of the School of Computing in their graduation process during the protocols phase.
    • What was made?:
  • I identified project objectives and requirements, ensuring that the final product met client expectations.
    I integrated databases using PostgreSQL in creating schemas, tables, and relationships for optimal storage structure.
