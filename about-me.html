About me

I help create intuitive, functional, and user-centered digital experiences by combining UX/UI design, frontend development, and database management.

I am passionate about user experience design and web development, with skills in tools and technologies such as React, Vite, PostgreSQL, and Figma. I enjoy tackling challenges that allow me to learn and apply innovative solutions, ensuring that each product is not only efficient but also accessible and aligned with user needs.

How do I contribute?

  • Identify objectives and user needs to ensure the final product meets client expectations.
  • Design attractive and functional interfaces through wireframes and prototypes in Figma, applying usability and visual design principles.
  • Develop optimized web applications with React and Vite, creating reusable components and enhancing the user experience.
  • Manage databases with PostgreSQL, designing efficient schemas for optimal data organization.
  • Provide clear and structured documentation to define processes, requirements, and ensure the quality of deliverables.
My approach is based on innovation and empathy, creating technological solutions that not only fulfill their purpose but also improve people's lives. I am always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow in the world of design and technology.
